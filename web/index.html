In Loving Memory of Renzo

A gentle giant, a loyal heart, our forever good boy

May 10, 2026

Our Faithful Companion

Renzo was our gentle giant - a German Shepherd with a heart as big as he was. He was loyal, patient, and endlessly kind, the kind of dog who wanted nothing more than to be close to the people he loved. Whether he was leaning his full weight against your leg, following you from room to room, or curling up at the foot of the bed, Renzo had a way of making every quiet moment feel safe and complete.

He shared most of his life with his brother Elliott, a bossy yellow Labrador who never let Renzo forget who was really in charge. Renzo took it all in stride - the stolen toys, the stolen bones, the stolen attention - with the unshakable patience of a true gentleman. Watching the two of them together, sharing a bed or a sunbeam or a treat, was one of the great joys of our home.

On May 10, 2026, Renzo passed away. He met the end the same way he met everything else in his life - quietly, bravely, and surrounded by love. He is rejoined now with Elliott, no doubt finally getting first pick of the toys.

Renzo asked for very little and gave everything. He will be missed for the rest of our lives, and remembered every single day. Good boy, Renzo. The very best boy.
